The Mission

Nyarugenge district has faced significant water scarcity issues for over a decade. This initiative, led by local leaders, aims to build a sustainable solar-powered water filtration system.

Your contributions go directly towards high-grade equipment and training local youth to maintain the system, ensuring longevity and community ownership.
